class hpotk.validate.RuleValidator

Bases: object

RuleValidator checks if a sequence of Identified or TermId items meet the validation requirements. The observation status is included in case the items extend ObservableFeature.

The validators can check each item individually or as a collection, for instance, to discover violation of the annotation propagation rule, etc.

The issues are returned as ValidationResults.
